## 👨‍💻 About the Project

This is my personal developer portfolio built to present my work, skills, and experience in a structured and visually appealing way. It serves as a central hub for anyone looking to learn more about me — whether you're a recruiter, collaborator, or just curious!

The portfolio highlights:
- Who I am and what I do
- Projects I've built with descriptions and links
- My technical skills and tools I work with
- My resume and professional background
- A way to get in touch with me

---

## ✨ Features

- 📱 **Fully Responsive** — Optimized for all screen sizes (mobile, tablet, desktop)
- ⚡ **Fast & Lightweight** — Clean codebase with optimized performance
- 🧑‍💻 **Projects Showcase** — Detailed cards with descriptions, tech used, and live/GitHub links
- 📄 **Resume Section** — Work experience, education, and skills at a glance
- 📬 **Contact Form** — Easy way for visitors to reach out directly
- 🎨 **Minimal UI** — Clean design focused on readability and user experience
- 🌙 **Smooth Animations** — Subtle transitions for a polished feel
